Video Results

We also provide video results, in which we showcase perceptual (labeled as TAA OFF) and perceptual TAA (labeled as TAA ON) results for two scenes.
Before playing the video, please make sure that:
  • You have an up-to-date video player. We recommend VLC Player.
  • Make sure that the player of your choice presents the videos as originally encoded. In case you are using VLC make sure that the interlacing option is turned off in the settings.
  • Make sure that the video is displayed as closely to 60FPS as possible.
  • You are watching the video from the correct distance from the monitor, which should be at least 2 times the height of the display. As you further move away from the screen you should see that our method flickers less and less, while the other two methods have the appearance of noise move around.
